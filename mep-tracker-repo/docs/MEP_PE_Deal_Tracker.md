## 02 — Top 5 Deals (Signal Over Volume)

**1. Bain Capital + Mubadala -> Service Logic — Recap (control, Dec 2025)**  
Control transaction in the largest independent U.S. commercial mechanical services platform.  
*Why it matters:* The defining commercial-MEP capital event of the window — top-tier sponsor plus sovereign-adjacent capital underwriting contracted commercial mechanical service revenue at national scale.

**2. ADIA + GIC (minority) -> Pye-Barker Fire & Safety — Recap (Jan 2025)**  
Altas and Leonard Green retained lead positions; two sovereign wealth funds took minority stakes. Platform then executed 57 acquisitions in 2025 (9,000 employees, 47 states).  
*Why it matters:* Sovereign wealth entry validates code-mandated recurring inspection revenue as institutional-grade; funds the fastest add-on cadence in commercial MEP.

**3. Blackstone -> AIR Control Concepts — Recap (sole institutional investor, 2026)**  
Blackstone acquired Madison Dearborn's remaining stake; founder/CEO reinvested. 38 operating companies across 35 states + Canada; stated data-center expansion focus; six add-ons YTD 2026 per Capstone.  
*Why it matters:* Mega-cap capital consolidating ownership of a commercial HVAC platform explicitly to pursue data-center cooling demand — the clearest end-market signal in commercial HVAC.

**4. Truelink Capital -> Horwitz — Platform (completed Jul 1, 2026)**  
Significant investment in a 1918-founded Minneapolis MEP services provider (HVAC/plumbing/electrical/BAS) serving data centers, semiconductor fab, medtech, healthcare, and advanced manufacturing; management retains significant ownership.  
*Why it matters:* Freshest platform in the window and a direct commercial/industrial mission-critical play — confirms sponsor appetite for MEP contractors tied to the data-center and semiconductor build-out is still accelerating into mid-2026.

**5. Stellex Capital -> ICS Holding — Platform (completed Jan 15, 2025)**  
NY middle-market sponsor acquired a mission-critical electrical/instrumentation services provider (Houston; Southeast US) serving industrial, healthcare, energy/power, institutional end markets; Langford Electric followed as add-on.  
*Why it matters:* Anchors the window's start with a clean commercial/industrial electrical platform formation that has already demonstrated the add-on thesis — the template for the mission-critical electrical roll-up.
